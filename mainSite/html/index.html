Effectively manage your photos!

Use your existing cloud storage.
More and more photos are ending up on cloud storage devices. Whether its Dropbox, Google Drive, Sky Drive, Amazon Cloud Drive or a combination, Photolude provides you with tools to browse photos easily and effectively.
You control your experience.
Install and use a variety of apps. Everything from how your photos are laid out, to tools which allow you to edit your photos can be installed into your experience at any time.
Share, collaborate, edit and search.
Searching your photos by date, sharing your photos with friend, and post pictures on social media are just a couple of examples of the ever expanding capabilities of Photolude. Just install the apps you want and you're off.
Do you want to contribute?
If there's a feature you would love, don't wait for it, build it and share it with others. Photolude is built on a web plugin platform which enables anyone who's interested to contribute after a few verification steps.
